Rules of Checkers

Each player has 12 pieces. They can only move forward one square to their left or right diagonal.

They can eliminate other pieces but jumping to the diagonal over them, and they can do this multiple times.

When a piece reaches the end of the board, they become kings and can move anywhere they want.
